Welcome

On December 18th, Netroots New York, Pace University and activists will host sessions about the Occupy movement. Whether you self-identify as an occupier or simply want to learn more about this emergent political phenomenon, we think this event will be a great way to spend you time; without the risk of doing time. Register to Attend

The Unconference

In addition to sessions organized by of organizing a conference where 'experts' create sessions for attendees, we're organizing an unconference in which participants are empowered to create their own sessions.

Half the sessions will be organized the morning of the event between 9am and 10am. The other half of the sessions will are being organized before the event by NYCGA Working Groups.

  • If you're involved in a working group you can apply to reserve a room for a session.
  • Food will not be provided. Please bring something for yourself, and something to share, if possible. Click here if you're interested in organizing breakfast/lunch support.
