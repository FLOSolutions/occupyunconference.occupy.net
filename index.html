Welcome

On December 18th, hundreds of folks interested in the Occupy movement will converge at Pace University to share skills and ideas. Whether you've occupied in the past or are curious about an occupied future, we think this event could be a great way to spent your time; without the threat of having to do time. Register to Attend

The Unconference

Instead of organizing a conference where 'experts' create sessions for attendees, we've organized an unconference in which participants are empowered to organize their own sessions.

Half of the sessions are the event will be organized beforehand by NYCGA Working Groups.

The other half of the sessions will be organized during the morning sessions the day of the event.
